Nokia 3330
2004 - 2005

When I wanted to call my parents to give them news I had to climb 200 meters above the cottage and hold my arm in the air to hope to get some bars of network.

My grandmother used to lend me this phone and ended up giving it to me. I pretty much only used it to play Snake. And other games. I remember using it when I was on vacation with her in her chalet in the Etivaz mountain pasture. When I wanted to call my parents to give them news I had to climb 200 meters above the chalet and reach up to get some bars of network.
